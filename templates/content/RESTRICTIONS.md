# Restrictions

## Success Criteria

<!-- The evaluator scores each iteration against these. Mark each as (hard) or (soft). -->

- [ ] (hard) Matches the specified format and length
- [ ] (hard) Tone is appropriate for the target audience
- [ ] (soft) Has a compelling hook or opening
- [ ] (soft) Includes concrete examples or data points
- [ ] (soft) Flows logically from introduction to conclusion

## Hard Constraints

<!-- MUST pass. A single failure means the iteration fails. -->

- Factual claims must be accurate and verifiable
- Content must be original (no copy-paste from sources)
- Must match the specified word count / length range

## Soft Constraints

<!-- Scored. Meeting more raises the score. Failing one doesn't block the iteration. -->

- Uses active voice and short paragraphs
- Includes relevant links or references where appropriate
- Has been proofread for grammar and spelling

## Out of Scope

- Do not modify files outside the declared scope
- Do not modify any files inside the specs/ directory
- Do not generate misleading or clickbait content
- Do not include personal opinions unless the format calls for it
