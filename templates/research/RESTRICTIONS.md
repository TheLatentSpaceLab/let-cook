# Restrictions

## Success Criteria

<!-- The evaluator scores each iteration against these. Mark each as (hard) or (soft). -->

- [ ] (hard) All claims are supported by cited sources
- [ ] (hard) Covers all subtopics listed in the Goal
- [ ] (soft) Includes at least 5 distinct, credible sources
- [ ] (soft) Provides a clear executive summary
- [ ] (soft) Identifies gaps or open questions in the research

## Hard Constraints

<!-- MUST pass. A single failure means the iteration fails. -->

- Every factual claim must have a citation or source link
- No fabricated sources or hallucinated references
- Report must be structured with clear sections and headings

## Soft Constraints

<!-- Scored. Meeting more raises the score. Failing one doesn't block the iteration. -->

- Sources are recent (within the last 2 years where applicable)
- Includes counterarguments or alternative viewpoints
- Writing is clear and accessible to a non-expert audience

## Out of Scope

- Do not modify files outside the declared scope
- Do not modify any files inside the specs/ directory
- Do not fabricate data or statistics
- Do not plagiarize — always paraphrase and cite
